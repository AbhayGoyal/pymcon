We believe in building a community as much as we believe in building code.

PyMCon 2020 is asynchronous first virtual conference for the Bayesian community

We believe that the PyMC project is more than just a codebase but also a community that is interested not only in statistical methods and code, but also in sharing knowledge and helping others, whether they be Bayesian veterans or new to the world of open source. Just as we actively maintain and better our code repositories project, the purpose of this conference is to elevate our community.

The goals of the conference are to:

  • Create a space and time for community members to meet each other and interact
  • Record and organize the human expertise and experience around PyMC
  • Help folks find ways to contribute to PyMC authentic to themselves
